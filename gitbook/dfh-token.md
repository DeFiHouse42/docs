# 9. The DFH Token

The DFH token is the **coordination layer** of the DeFi House ecosystem.

## 9.1 Utility
- Fee discounts for staking (30–50%)  
- Priority order book placement  
- Enhanced borrower terms (higher LTV / lower fees)  
- Reputation multipliers  
- Cross-chain settlement priority  
- Governance rights  

## 9.2 Value Accrual
- 60–70% of protocol fees → DFH buyback/burn or staker rewards  
- 100% of insurance premiums → DFH stakers  
- Burn events tied to penalties and fees  
- Exposure to protocol usage via staking  

## 9.3 Governance
DFH holders govern:
- Collateral listings  
- LTV parameters  
- Fee structures  
- Insurance mechanics  
- Cross-chain expansion
