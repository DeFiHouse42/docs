# Mission & Vision

## Mission
To make decentralized finance **practical**, **understandable**, and **empowering** through *clear education* and *responsible protocol design*.

## Vision
A **decentralized credit ecosystem** where users:
- *Understand* the mechanisms they interact with  
- Access **predictable** and **transparent** borrowing  
- Benefit from *sustainable incentives*  
- Participate in *responsible governance*  

## Principles

### Education first
Users should **understand the risks and mechanics** behind on-chain credit.

### Predictability
**Fixed-term, fixed-rate lending** avoids *unexpected liquidations* and *variable rates*.

### Responsible risk management
*Conservative collateral choices* and **transparent settlement rules**.

### Incremental expansion
Start with a **single-chain MVP**, expand cross-chain after *proven stability*.
