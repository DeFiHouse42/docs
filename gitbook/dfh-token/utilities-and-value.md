# Utilities & Value Accrual

## Utilities

### Fee Reductions
**Stakers** may receive reduced:
- *Origination fees*  
- *Early repayment fees*  
- *Order adjustment fees*  

### Lender Priority
**Staked lenders** may appear *earlier in borrower searches*.

### Enhanced Borrow Terms
**DFH staking** may unlock:
- **Higher maximum LTV**  
- Access to *premium offers*  
- **Lower penalties**  

### Reputation Influence
Staking may support **borrower reputation growth**.

### Cross-Chain Priority (Future)
Stakers may receive **reduced cross-chain execution fees**.

---

## Value Accrual

### Protocol Fees
A portion of **protocol fees** may go toward:
- **DFH buyback and burn**  
- **Distribution to stakers**  
- *Hybrid mechanisms*  

### Insurance Premiums (Future)
If *optional insurance products* are introduced:
- Premiums may accrue to **staking pools**  

{% hint style="danger" %}
These mechanisms describe potential token flow structures, not performance expectations.
{% endhint %}
