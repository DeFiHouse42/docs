# Community

The **DeFi House community** combines *in-person engagement* with *online participation*, creating a **consistent learning environment**.

## Workshops
**Hands-on sessions** focused on:
- *Wallets*, *transactions*, and *security basics*  
- **Liquidity**, **yield**, and **lending fundamentals**  
- *Understanding collateral and risk*  
- Using **on-chain tools** with real examples  

Workshops serve as an **entry point** for many community members.

## X (Twitter) Spaces
**Weekly discussions** covering:
- *DeFi trends and updates*  
- **Lending and credit concepts**  
- *Protocol breakdowns*  
- *Interviews with builders*  

These grow the reach of DeFi House **beyond local workshops**.

## Telegram Community Calls
**Regular voice calls** used for:
- *Open Q&A*  
- *Market discussions*  
- **Reviewing protocol concepts**  
- *Sharing feedback*  

The calls maintain **consistent engagement** and *collective learning*.
