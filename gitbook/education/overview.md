# Education Overview

Education is the foundation of DeFi House. The goal is to provide clarity, structure, and accessible learning that prepares users to engage responsibly with decentralized finance.

Current educational efforts include:
- In-person workshops  
- Weekly X (Twitter) Spaces  
- Weekly Telegram community calls  
- Community-driven discussions and Q&A  

These activities shape the protocol by surfacing practical questions, user challenges, and real-world needs.
