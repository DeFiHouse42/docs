# 2. Mission and Vision

## Mission
To make decentralized finance **accessible, practical, and empowering** by combining:

- High-quality education  
- Community collaboration  
- Thoughtful, user-centric DeFi products  

## Vision
A world where financial literacy and on-chain participation are universal, enabling individuals to:

- Understand DeFi mechanisms  
- Access transparent on-chain credit  
- Deploy capital safely and knowledgeably  
- Participate in the governance of open systems  

DeFi House aims to become a leading hub for **DeFi credit education and cross-chain P2P lending**.
